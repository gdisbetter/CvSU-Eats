Eatery Locator is a website designed by Cavite State University (CvSU) students, for CvSU students, to help easily find food spots around campus. Whether you're craving a quick snack or a full meal, our platform provides up-to-date information on nearby eateries, including menus, prices, and locations. With student-friendly features and recommendations, it’s your go-to guide for satisfying campus cravings.

What is our website about?

We are a team of dedicated Cavite State University students who created Eatery Locator to make campus life more convenient and delicious. As fellow students, we understand the struggle of finding affordable and accessible food options between classes. Our mission is to connect the CvSU community with the best local eateries around campus through a simple, user-friendly platform. Eatery Locator is built with student needs in mind — by students, for students.
